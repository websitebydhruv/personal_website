Hi

Sir Tim Berners-Lee invented the World Wide Web in 1989 Sir Tim is the Director of the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C), a web standards organisation in 1994. Which develops interoperable technologies (specifications,guidlines,software and tools) to lead the Web to it's full potential. He is a Director of the World Wide Web Foundation which was launched in 2009 to coordinate efforts to the further potential to the Web to benifit humanity.

In 2001 he became a Fellow of the Royal Society . He has been the recipient of several international rewards including the Japan Prize, The Prince Of Asturias Prize Foundation, The Millenium Technology Prize, The Germany's Die Quadriga award. in 2004 he was Knighted by H.M Queen Alizabeth and in 2007 he was awarded the Order of Merit. in 2009 he was elected a foreign associate of the National Academy of Sciences.
